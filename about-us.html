We Deliver the Most Advanced Research & Consutling Services

HydroSensing is the joint effort of researchers and academicians as well as industrail professionals from diverse backgrounds with expertise in Water Resources Engineering and Management, Hydraulic Engineering, Hydro-geology, Hydrological modeling, Remote Sensing and GIS applications in Hydrology, Geotechnical Engineering and Climate Change. The main aim of this platform is to share knowledge in general and especially to educate the community regarding water resources issues and propose plans to tackle these issues. The research contributions from our team members will be shared and insights will be provided regarding the current challenges related to impact of the increasing influence of climate change on water resources.

HydroSensing also aims to provide materials related to Machine Learning applications in Hydrology, Remote Sensing and GIS, Google Earth Engine codes, and other related software used in hydrological modeling. Moreover, the team has capacity to perform the consultancy services and has already successfully completed several research projects within the Indus basin Pakistan (and the services can be extended to any region of the world). We hope that HydroSensing will play its role in educating the early career researchers, and would be a useful platform for community development.

The HydroSensing will share useful information related to hydrological engineering, water resoruce management and climate change etc for researchers working in hydrology,remote sensing, water resources and hydraulic engineering, GIS and Remote Sensing applications, geo-hazards, and geotechnical engineering. The target audiences for this platform are Undergraduate, Master and Ph.D. students and early career researchers working in the aforementioned research domains. Besides sharing the research-oriented information, this platform will also provide information regarding Master, Ph.D., and Postdoc opportunities in different countries around the world.
