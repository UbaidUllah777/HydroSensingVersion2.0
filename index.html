ABOUT US

Cutting-edge Global Water Resource Engineering and Management Research and Consulting Services.

HydroSensing is a collaboration of experts with various backgrounds in Water Resources Engineering and Management, Hydraulic Engineering, Hydro-geology, Hydrological modeling, Remote Sensing, GIS applications in Hydrology, Geotechnical Engineering, and Climate Change. The platform aims to share knowledge and educate the community on water resource issues, and offer plans to address them. Our team will contribute research and provide insights on current challenges, particularly the impact of climate change on water resources.
